The Loremaster

16 April 2020 · 1 min · 78 words · Me

Bug dolu quest’ler, çalışmayan script’ler, işe yaramayan çöp quest gear’ı, 2005 yılından beri kıyıda köşede kalmış unutulmuş hikayeler…Ve sonunda World of Warcraft -The Loremaster- achievement’ı…

I am the bone of loremaster.
Quest book is my body, and lore is my blood.
I have completed over a thousand quests.
Unknown to death.
Nor known to life.
Have withstood pain to complete so many quests.
Yet, those hands will never achieve anything.
So as I pray, “Unlimited Quest Works.” 🔗
